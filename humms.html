⚖️ HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences)

HUMSS is for students curious about society, culture, and human behavior. They study subjects like Psychology, Sociology, Philippine Politics, Creative Writing, and World Religions. Students develop communication, research, and leadership skills.

Examples: Writing an article, conducting a survey, organizing a community project, or analyzing social issues.

Best for: Future teachers, lawyers, journalists, social workers, psychologists, and communicators.

Goal: To build empathy, critical thinking, and strong communication skills while understanding society.
